Practice Area

Debt Recovery, General Litigation, FinancialMis-selling, Personal & Corporate Insolvency

Debt Recovery

We are able to help individuals and companies Recover unpaid debts quickly, secure judgment orders for unpaid debts, enforce sale orders and obtain charging orders.

General Litigation

We can assist with contractual disputes, commence proceedings, draft pleadings and prepare for and attend mediation or without prejudice meeting to facilitate out of court settlements.

FinanicalMis-selling

AMH solicitors are able to provide expert guidance if you have been mis-sold any financial products; commence legal proceedings; prepare evidence to support any mis-sold derivatives including finding the right experts and forensic accountants to build a strong claim; and provide representation at mediation to achieve optimal out of court settlements.

Personal & Corporate Insolvency


AMH solicitors are able to provide assistance with issuing bankruptcy petitions or winding up petitions. We are able to assist in setting aside statutory demands. We can assist in making bankruptcy annulment applications. We can also assist in defending winding up petitions or bankruptcy petitions brought by HMRC or any other large or even smaller creditors.
